# Banner prompt — for Claude (image generation / "Claude design")

Paste this as-is. Target output: a wide GitHub README banner,
**~1500×600 px (2.5:1)**, saved to `docs/banner.png`.

---

**Prompt:**

> A cinematic, noir-poster-style banner for a developer tool called
> **ROBIN** — an AI voice sidekick that makes the dreaded phone calls
> for you. Wide 2.5:1 hero composition.
>
> Subject: a single vintage rotary desk telephone, reimagined as a
> vigilante's tool — matte black, sculptural, slightly oversized,
> rim-lit. The coiled handset cord trails off-frame and dissolves into a
> thin glowing waveform / sound-wave line, as if the call itself is
> alive. One small hot ember of light glows where the dial meets the
> base.
>
> Mood: hardboiled, confident, after-hours. Equal parts noir detective
> office and modern terminal. It should feel like the calm before Robin
> wins an argument.
>
> Palette: near-black charcoal background (#0b0b0d) with a single
> aggressive accent — hot signal-orange (#ff5c00) — used sparingly for
> the glow, the waveform, and a thin underline. One cool secondary:
> faint electric teal in the deepest shadows. No other colors.
>
> Typography baked into the art: the wordmark **"ROBIN"** set large in a
> heavy condensed grotesque or a sharp slab — confident, slightly
> brutalist, letter-spaced wide — lower-left or centered. Beneath it, in
> small mono-spaced caps: *"PHONE YOUR AGENT. IT MAKES THE CALL YOU
> HATE."* Keep type crisp and legible at small sizes.
>
> Texture & finish: subtle 35mm film grain, a faint halftone/print
> texture, soft vignette, gentle bloom on the orange. Dramatic single
> light source, deep shadows, high contrast, shallow depth of field.
> Generous negative space on one side for breathing room.
>
> Avoid: stock-photo realism, busy collages, multiple bright colors,
> purple/blue tech gradients, glassmorphism, emoji, clip-art robots,
> generic "AI" iconography, cluttered backgrounds. One bold idea,
> executed with restraint.

---

**Variations to try if the first isn't right:**

- *Tighter:* drop the phone, make it pure typographic — giant "ROBIN"
  filling the frame, the O replaced by a glowing dial, everything else
  black.
- *Warmer:* swap signal-orange for hot amber (#ffb000), add a faint
  scanline/CRT texture for a retro-futurist read.
- *Colder:* acid-green (#39ff14) accent on true black for a more
  hacker/terminal tone.

Export at 2x for retina (3000×1200), then place at `docs/banner.png`.
